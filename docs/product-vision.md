# Product Vision: LifePlanner

## Elevator Pitch
LifePlanner is a centralized hub that bridges the gap between time management and life management. It enhances your standard calendar by letting you easily drag and drop your tasks, chores, and ideas directly onto your schedule.

## The Core Problem
Life planning is highly fragmented. People use calendars to track *time*, note-taking apps to store *ideas*, and task managers to track *chores*. This separation makes it difficult to turn inspiration and tasks into scheduled action.

## Target Audience
*   Busy individuals trying to organize personal projects and routines.
*   Couples coordinating date nights and shared responsibilities.
*   Families managing household chores and group events.

## Key Value Proposition
*   **Drag-and-Drop Scheduling:** Transform static ideas into actionable scheduled events simply by dragging a contextual card onto your calendar.
*   **Active Inspiration:** The app acts as an active inspirator, using AI to suggest events, activities, and date nights when you have free time.
*   **Frictionless Planning:** Reduce the friction of planning routines and shared responsibilities.
