# Product Vision: Schedulist

## Elevator Pitch
Schedulist is a centralized planning workstation that bridges the gap between structured time management (calendars) and unstructured task management (checklists, ideas, and chores). It replaces fragmented tools by allowing you to organize your goals into cards and drag-and-drop individual checklist items directly onto your daily calendar grid.

## The Core Problem
Life planning is highly fragmented. People use calendars to track *time*, note-taking apps to store *ideas*, and task managers to track *chores*. This separation makes it difficult to turn inspiration and tasks into scheduled action. 

By keeping lists and calendars in separate silos, users either over-commit because their tasks lack time constraints, or they schedule events manually on their calendars, creating rigid, static entries that are difficult to update.

## Target Audience
*   **Busy Individuals** trying to organize personal projects, habits, and routines.
*   **Couples** coordinating schedules, date nights, and shared responsibilities.
*   **Families** managing household chores, routines, and group events.

## Key Value Proposition
*   **The Thought-to-Time Bridge:** Seamless drag-and-drop scheduling that transforms static items on checklist cards into active calendar blocks.
*   **Multi-Instance Scheduling:** Drag a single task item to multiple calendar dates and complete each occurrence independently.
*   **Active Inspiration:** Uses integrated AI assistance to recommend routines, chores, and activities when gaps appear in your calendar.
*   **Universal Integration:** Direct bi-directional synchronization with Google Calendar, Google Tasks, and Microsoft To-Do to centralize your entire workflow.
